GRAN NEVADO

We have been producing the ADENTRO Malbec and Merlot since 2011. Four years later, we have added to our selection, the white wine ADENTRO Torrontés. These elegant wines with intense fruit flavors are vinified close to our vineyard in one of Argentina’s most modern wineries. The special climate allows the creation of intense, complex wines, rich in fruit flavors, accompanied by soft tannins. Our vineyard is one of the highest in the world, situated at almost 2700 meters above sea level.
As of summer 2017 our wines are also available in Switzerland.
