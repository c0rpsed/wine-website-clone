Malbec

Intense, dark ruby red color. Expressive bouquet of rich, ripe violet fruit with a floral scent and hints of minerality. Discreet touch of tobacco and leather. Fresh acidity balanced with soft, round tannins. The unique terroir of Cachi is embodied in this wine. Long and noble finish.


Country of origin:

Cachi Adentro, Salta, Argentina

Alcohol:

14.5%

Aging:

12 months in oak

Complements

Roast, red meats, barbeque, and stews
