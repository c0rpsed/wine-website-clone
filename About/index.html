About Us

Attracted by the majestic mountains of the Andes, Viera Merz and ChristianxGötz made several journeys to the northwest of Argentina.

Fascinated by the mountains and the pristine landscape, in July 2005 we decided to purchase land in Cachi Adentro, at the foot of the Nevado de Cachi.

ChristianzGötz

History

After acquiring the land, we put all our energy and passion into developing the infrastructure of the farm. In stages we planted altogether 8.25 hectares of vines and a walnut orchard measuring 1.5 hectares. With the invaluable help of three employees we built up the finca from scratch. So now, we are proud of our six hectares of Malbec, one hectare of Merlot and one and a half hectares of Torrontés vines. We have our grapes vinified into elegant wines by a modern winery nearby.

Our next project is to build our own “bodega”. We seize this opportunity despite the challenges of building a winery in an isolated region.

Since the summer of 2017 we are exporting the ADENTRO wines to Switzerland, and we are delighted with the positive feedback.
